Privacy Policy

Your data, your trust — we protect both with transparency and care.

Our commitment

MuttCulture (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us offline. We adhere to the principles of data minimization, purpose limitation, and accountability.

We are proud to be compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy frameworks. Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) oversees all privacy-related matters and can be reached at dpo@muttculture.com.

Information we collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, as well as data generated through your interactions with our services. The categories of personal data include:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, postal address, and company name (if applicable).
  • Account credentials: username, password, and security questions.
  • Payment information: billing address, payment method details (processed through secure third-party gateways; we do not store full payment card data).
  • Service usage: product preferences, service history, support tickets, and feedback.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies & tracking: we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site performance (see our Cookies Policy).

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., race, religion, health data) unless explicitly provided by you for a specific purpose (e.g., allergy information for product safety).

How we use your data

Your personal data is used for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our products and services.
  • To process transactions and send order confirmations.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and educational content (you may opt out at any time).
  • To personalize your experience and recommend relevant solutions.
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms of service.
  • To conduct analytics and aggregate research to enhance our offerings.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Your trust is our priority.

Data sharing & disclosure

We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as:

  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal (your payment data is encrypted and never stored on our servers).
  • Shipping partners: FedEx, UPS, and local couriers for order fulfillment.
  • Cloud infrastructure: AWS and Google Cloud for hosting and data storage.
  • Analytics tools: Google Analytics and Hotjar (anonymized data for performance analysis).

We require all third-party processors to adhere to strict data protection standards and only process data for specified purposes. In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale), we will notify you and give you the option to withdraw your data.

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of MuttCulture, our users, or others.

Your rights & choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Opt out of direct marketing and certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@muttculture.com. We will respond within 30 days. For verified requests, we may ask for identification to protect your privacy.

Children's privacy

MuttCulture does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). Our services are directed to adults and pet care professionals. If we become aware of data collected from a child, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with data should contact us at privacy@muttculture.com.

Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via our website is secured with TLS 1.3.
  • Access controls: Role-based access and multi-factor authentication for internal systems.
  • Regular audits: We conduct vulnerability scans and penetration testing annually.
  • Data minimization: We store only what is necessary and retain it for as long as required for business or legal purposes.

While no system is 100% secure, we continuously update our protocols to address emerging threats. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and regulatory authorities within 72 hours as required by law.

International data transfers

MuttCulture operates globally, and your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States and the European Economic Area. We ensure that such transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved mechanisms. For transfers to third-party processors, we require equivalent safeguards.

Data retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. For example:

  • Account data: Retained until you close your account, plus 30 days for recovery.
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years (tax and accounting requirements).
  • Marketing preferences: Retained until you opt out, then promptly deleted.

After the retention period, your data is securely anonymized or deleted.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will post the revised version on this page with an updated “Last Revised” date. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or through a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
