Cookies Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on MuttCulture.

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide valuable information to website owners. Cookies can be "session cookies" (which are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

At MuttCulture, we use cookies to understand how you interact with our website, remember your preferences, and deliver personalized content. We also use cookies for analytics, security, and marketing purposes. This policy explains the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

We respect your privacy. You have the right to accept or decline cookies, and you can change your preferences at any time.

Types of cookies we use

We use the following categories of cookies on our website:

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as they are required for the website to operate.
  • Preference cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference, region, or login details) to provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Analytics cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and how users navigate the site. This data helps us improve our website and user experience. We use Google Analytics and other tools for this purpose.
  • Marketing cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements to you based on your interests and browsing behavior. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an ad and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Why we use cookies

Cookies enable us to provide you with a seamless and personalized experience on MuttCulture. Specifically, we use cookies to:

  • Improve website performance: Analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with our site, allowing us to optimize content and functionality.
  • Remember your preferences: Preference cookies save your settings so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit.
  • Provide relevant content: Marketing cookies allow us to show you products and services that may interest you based on your browsing history.
  • Enhance security: Essential cookies help us protect your account and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Facilitate transactions: Session cookies are necessary for processing orders and maintaining your shopping cart.

Managing your cookie preferences

You have the right to control how cookies are used on your device. Here are the ways you can manage your cookie preferences:

  • Cookie consent banner: When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer or by clearing your browser cookies.
  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie settings through their preferences menu. You can block or delete cookies, or set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being placed. Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
  • Third-party opt-out tools: You can opt out of interest-based advertising from third-party providers through the Network Advertising Initiative (optout.networkadvertising.org) or the Digital Advertising Alliance (optout.aboutads.info).

We respect your choices. If you decline cookies, we will only use essential cookies necessary for the website to function.

Cookie retention periods

The retention period for cookies varies depending on their purpose. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a fixed period, ranging from a few hours to several years. The specific retention periods for our cookies are listed in the table above. We periodically review our cookie usage to ensure that we retain data only as long as necessary.

You can manually delete cookies from your browser at any time. Please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions on how to clear cookies and other browsing data.

Third-party cookies

We work with trusted third-party partners who may set cookies on our website. These partners include:

  • Google: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and user behavior. Google may also use cookies for advertising purposes. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Facebook (Meta): We use Facebook Pixel to track conversions and deliver targeted advertisements. You can learn more at facebook.com/privacy.
  • Payment processors: Our payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal) may set cookies to facilitate secure transactions. Their privacy policies apply.
  • Social media platforms: If you share our content on social media, those platforms may set cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies.

We encourage you to review the privacy and cookie policies of these third-party providers to understand how they use your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal obligations, or the cookies we use. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may notify you via email or through a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
