Science behind the scent

Odors are volatile organic compounds (VOCs) released by bacteria, urine, feces, and organic decay. Traditional air fresheners mask these smells, but MuttCulture’s enzymatic approach actually digests the proteins and fatty acids that cause them. Our blends include protease, amylase, and lipase enzymes that target specific odor precursors.

We also incorporate activated bamboo charcoal and plant-based essential oils to absorb and neutralize residual smells. The result is a dual-action system: biological breakdown plus physical adsorption. Independent lab tests show a 98.7% reduction in ammonia and skatole within 60 minutes of application.
